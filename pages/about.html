Paniolo is the Hawaiian word for cowboy — ranchers who have herded cattle across the slopes of Mauna Kea since the 1830s. We herd your coding agents with the same precision: structured, purposeful, and built to last. We're proud to build this from Honolulu, Hawaiʻi.

What We Believe

The principles behind
Paniolo

Every AI error is infrastructure debt.

When an agent makes a mistake, the right response isn't to fix the output. It's to update the harness so the same mistake is structurally prevented forever.

Harness quality is model-agnostic.

A well-engineered harness outperforms a better base model with a weak one. Empirically validated. Not a theory — a result from ICLR 2026.

Observability before optimization.

You cannot improve what you cannot see. Before any harness evolves, every component, trajectory, and decision must be auditable.
